About this planner

Formula data is summarised from Handmade Sourdough: From Starter to Baked Loaf. Pain au Levain final dough water is intentionally adjusted from the source value of 400 g to your preferred 370 g for the base two-loaf batch.

Hydration is an estimate including preferment build water and flour. Always check the actual dough temperature after mixing and adjust the next bake if needed.

Your settings and completed steps are stored only in this browser.
